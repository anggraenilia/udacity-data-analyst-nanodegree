Analysis of WeRateDogs Twitter Data¶

WeRateDogs is a twitter account that gives a rating of dogs. I have been wrangling to get data from WeRateDogs tweets from 2015 to 2017. In addition, I got data that contained the 3 highest prediction results for dog breeds from images posted by WeRateDogs. The amount of data obtained was 1994 data.

The day and time (24 hour) when a tweet in a post has a very small correlation with the favorite count and retweet count. Day and time correlation to favorite count respectively 0.045 and 0.057 while day and time correlation to retweet count are 0.045 and 0.057. The small correlation shows that the day and time when the tweet is posted has very little effect on the favorite count and retweet count, and even has no effect.
